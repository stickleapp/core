Usage Guidelines
  • Use the red logo on light or white backgrounds.
  • Use the white (dark mode) logo on dark or black backgrounds.
  • Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the icon.
  • Do not alter the colors, proportions, or opacity of the logo elements.
  • Do not place the light logo on dark backgrounds or vice versa.
  • Minimum display width: 120px for the full logo, 24px for the icon.
